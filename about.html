About Senko

Where fragrance becomes presence

Our Story

Senko is not a store. It is atmosphere. It is memory. It is silence shaped into scent.

We emerged from a simple belief: that fragrance should be experienced as art. Each bottle we curate tells a story of Korean craftsmanship, where tradition meets contemporary minimalism.

As an authorized retailer of Senko, we bring their visionary approach to fragrance to discerning collectors worldwide. Our spaces in Seoul, New York, and Paris are designed as sanctuaries—quiet places where you can discover scent in its purest form.

Curation

Every fragrance is carefully selected for its artistic merit, craftsmanship, and emotional resonance.

Experience

We create environments where fragrance can be discovered slowly, mindfully, without distraction.

Authenticity

As authorized Senko retailers, we guarantee the provenance and quality of every bottle.
